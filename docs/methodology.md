# ARAM Cognitive Engine — Methodology

---

## Core Approach

ARAM is not a linear method.

It is a **cognitive process designed to operate under pressure**, where:

- information is incomplete  
- actors are irrational  
- outcomes are uncertain  

---

## Strategic Matrix

Every scenario is analyzed through five dimensions:

### 1. Power
Who controls critical variables?

### 2. Interest
Who benefits or loses?

### 3. Fear
What is not being said?

### 4. Illusion
What narratives distort reality?

### 5. Hidden Opportunity
What remains unseen?

---

## Decision Logic

ARAM operates under the following principles:

- clarity precedes action  
- contradictions reveal structure  
- simulation prevents naive execution  
- strategy must be executable, not theoretical  

---

## AI Integration

AI is used to:

- expand perception  
- challenge assumptions  
- simulate alternatives  

But:

> AI does not decide — humans do.

---

## Expected Output

Each process results in:

- a clear diagnosis  
- a strategic direction  
- an actionable plan  
- risk awareness  

---

## Method Constraint

ARAM avoids:

- generic frameworks  
- static models  
- purely rational assumptions  

---

## Final Principle

> Decisions fail not due to lack of intelligence,  
> but due to misreading reality.
